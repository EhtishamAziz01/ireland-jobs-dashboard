# Data Jobs Market — Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

Analysis of **1,930 real LinkedIn job postings** for data-related roles reveals that **SQL (30.5%)**, **Excel (30.1%)**, and **Python (23.1%)** are the most in-demand skills. Data Scientists command the highest average salary at **$160,068**, followed by Data Engineers at **$152,394**, with Data Analysts at **$110,658**.

## Recommendations for Job Seekers

1. **Must-have skills**: SQL, Excel, Python — these three cover the broadest set of opportunities
2. **Differentiate with**: Scala, Machine Learning, ETL pipeline experience
3. **Don't neglect soft skills**: Communication and cross-functional collaboration appear in 24% and 14% of postings respectively
4. **Salary optimization**: Upskilling from Data Analyst → Data Engineer or Data Scientist yields a $40K–$50K salary increase on average
5. **Target companies**: Focus on companies with multiple data postings — they have structured data teams and clearer career paths
